Add to Home Screen (iOS only)

In order to have a light load on our users' storage devices, we have allowed the ability to add Geoweather to your home screen. This can be achieved by following these steps:

First, open the Weather section.

At the bottom of your screen, tap the "Share" icon (looks like a square with an arrow pointing up).

Look for the option that says "Add to Home Screen."

Tap on that option, and then tap "Add."

You should now have Geoweather added to your home screen, costing minimal storage on your device. :)
