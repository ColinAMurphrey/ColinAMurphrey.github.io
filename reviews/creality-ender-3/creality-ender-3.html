Creality Ender-3 Review

The Creality Ender-3 is sort of a meme at this point in time, but that doesn't mean it's the worst printer out there. In fact, during my time owning this printer, I can definitely say that this printer is still a great value if you're wanting to get into 3D printing. While it's no Bambu Lab A1 Mini, it's still a great printer to learn on.
